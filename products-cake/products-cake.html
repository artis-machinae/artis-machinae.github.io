What We Do


Wedding Cakes

Our highly experienced bakers will assist you in designing the perfect cake for your wedding, from appearance to flavor and everything in between

Birthday Cakes

What better way to celebrate the birth of that special someone than with a custom cake? We do everything from multi-tier cakes to cake pops and cupcakes

Bakery Shop

Come on in to our shop to satisfy that craving for delicious cake. We have multiple flavors every day, and offer to-go boxes so you can have a piece tomorrow, or share with someone at home.

We also offer all sorts of delectable pastries and baked goods including macarons, croissants, petits fours, and more!
