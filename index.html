About

tl;dr:

I like making things. I'd love to help you make things too.

More information:

Perhaps it's inevitable that the child who spends extra time at school in the computer lab, disassembles various small electronics trying to fix them, and thinks staying at the library until closing time sounds like a great Friday night will grow up to make a living in the tech industry.

Due to my incessant need to know how things work, I went on to teach myself HTML 4.0 back in the good ol' days of using notepad and hitting "refresh" a lot. This of course led to CSS, JavaScript, and more. I am positively thrilled with what everything has evolved into.

Over two decades in tech has given me great insight and appreciation of both what's happening behind the scenes, and the front-end user experience.

In my spare time, I like creating random code projects for fun, building computers, fixing electronics, making music, working on cars, and various DIY house projects.
